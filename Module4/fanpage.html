I Built the Sky Tribute Page

I Built the Sky is a one-man guitar instrumental project initiated since 2012 by Australian guitarist Rohan Stevenson.

Albums picks

  • I Built the Sky (2012)

    • This self titled album has a great mix of chill acoustic works ("Narcrous"), melodic riffs("Mammatus") and sick djents ("Opacus").

  • The Sky Is Not the Limit (2016)

    • In this album Stevenson collaborates with other great guitarists such as Jake Lowe, Stephen Taranto, and Sithu Aye in "Stratiformis", "Radiatus" and "Translucidus" that exhibits high technical competence in various solos as well as tasteful groovy riffs.

  • The Zenith Rises

    • One of my all time favorite. This album has some high quality tunes such as "Up into the Ether" and "Wormhole Traveler" which features heavenly melodies and smooth transitions. Pure eargasm.
